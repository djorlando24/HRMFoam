# HRMFoam 3D case

Flashing water in an expanding pipe - similar to the "Moby Dick" experiment.
Water enters at 1.38bar, 373K.
Saturated water-steam mixture leaves at 25kPa.
Isenthalpic boundary conditions.

Initial condition is saturated steam mixture at the back pressure.
Inlet pressure ramps up over time to 1.38 bar. (like opening a valve upstream)
